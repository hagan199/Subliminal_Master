# Subliminal Master - Business Document

## Company / Product Name
**Subliminal Master v4.0** — Desktop Affirmation Flashing App

---

## Executive Summary

Subliminal Master is a cross-platform desktop application that flashes positive affirmations on your screen below conscious perception. It targets the growing self-improvement and manifestation community by providing a passive, "set and forget" personal development tool that works while you do other things.

---

## Problem Statement

The self-improvement market is booming ($13.2 billion globally), but:

- **Active effort barrier** — Meditation, journaling, and affirmation practice require dedicated time
- **Consistency struggle** — People start affirmation routines but abandon them within weeks
- **Expensive alternatives** — Subliminal audio programs cost $50-200+ per program
- **Limited customization** — Pre-made subliminal products don't let you write your own messages
- **No verification** — Audio subliminals are invisible — users can't confirm messages are actually there
- **Platform lock-in** — Most subliminal tools are mobile-only or web-only

---

## Solution

Subliminal Master provides a free, open-source desktop application that:

1. **Flashes text on screen** — Messages appear for 10-500ms, below conscious reading speed
2. **4 flash effects** — Instant, Fade In, Glow Pulse, Typewriter
3. **5 focus zones** — Full screen, top half, bottom half, center, corners
4. **Vision board** — Flash images alongside text (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP)
5. **Breathing sync** — Messages flash during exhale phase for receptivity
6. **Power Hour** — Rapid-fire mode for intense sessions
7. **Night mode** — Warm amber colors for sleep sessions
8. **Stealth mode** — Hide the app, keep subliminals running
9. **Custom messages** — Add, edit, import, and export your own affirmations
10. **Preset packs** — Wealth, Confidence, Health, Love, Career bundles
11. **Auto-start** — Launch on system startup
12. **Stats dashboard** — Track streak days, total flashes, session count

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Subliminal messaging is pseudoscience | High | Position as "affirmation tool", not medical/therapeutic |
| Antivirus flagging .exe downloads | Medium | Code-sign the executable, provide source code for trust |
| Small niche market | Medium | Cross-market to productivity/study/focus communities |
| Users concerned about screen safety | Low | Clear documentation on flash durations, test mode |
| Open source = no revenue | Low | Freemium model — free core, paid premium content |
